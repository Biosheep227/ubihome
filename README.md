# UbiLLM Home: Truly Smart Homes with Fine-Grained Sensing and Logical AI

> **Note:** The full paper and source code will be released soon! Star this repository to stay updated. ⭐

## 🏠 Why Current Smart Homes Aren't "Smart" Enough?

Most existing smart home systems face two major bottlenecks:
1. **"Blind" Sensors:** Traditional sensors are too basic (coarse-grained). They can't capture the fine details of your daily activities, making true personalized control impossible.
2. **Rigid AI (Pattern Matching):** Current home automation methods rely on supervised learning, which is just rigid pattern matching. They don't "think" logically like humans, nor can they automatically adapt when your living habits change over time.

## 💡 Introducing UbiLLM Home

We propose **UbiLLM**, a zero-code smart home platform that combines **fine-grained, ubiquitous sensing** with the reasoning power of **Large Language Models (LLMs)**.

Instead of relying on rigid rules or manual voice commands, UbiLLM understands your context and acts logically. We designed a unique **Dual-Agent LLM System**:
* 🤖 **The Actor Agent:** Observes your fine-grained activities and automatically generates smart home routines (Trigger-Action Programs) using human-like logic.
* 🧠 **The Learner Agent:** Listens to your feedback and continuously updates your personal preference knowledge base (via RAG). It grows and adapts with you over time.

## 🚀 Key Highlights

- **Evolves with You:** Unlike traditional AI, UbiLLM continuously adapts to your changing habits without requiring new training data.
- **Highly Accurate:** Improves automation accuracy by **20.3%** and user satisfaction by **28.3%** compared to baseline methods.
- **Cost-Effective:** Our dual-agent design splits the workload, reducing LLM token consumption by a massive **69.3%**.
- **Human-Like Logic:** Moves beyond simple pattern matching to actual context-aware reasoning.
